    Can guinea pigs have bacon?

    Meat of any kind. That means no cat, dog, or ferret food either! Guinea pig digestive systems are not set up to digest anything but plants. They don't have the GI tract equipment to digest any meat at all, and it can make them quite seriously ill.

    Can guinea pigs eat peanut butter?

    Peanut butter should never be given to guinea pigs – not even as a rare treat. The thick texture makes it a choking hazard. Guinea pigs can't easily digest the fat, sugar, and preservatives in peanut butter. Additionally, the calories and additives will make make guinea pigs overweight.

    Can guinea pigs eat cheerios?

    I would not recommend it. The fact that they are whole grain products and processed rules them out. It is not recommended for guinea pigs to eat grains or processed foods at all.

    Can guinea pig eat potato chips?

    No, guinea pigs shouldn't eat potato chips. Potato chips are deep-fried and contain too much salt. These ingredients are too complex for the guinea pig's digestive system to digest, so avoid potato chips at all costs. The same case applies to French fries.

    Can guinea pigs digest milk?

    Guinea pigs are herbivorous creatures, and because of that, it is important to avoid ever feeding them any dairy products, including milk, according to the Humane Society of Huron Valley. The furry rodents are lactose intolerant, which means that their bodies simply do not digest dairy items sufficiently or adequately.

    What kills a guinea pig?

    Vitamin C deficiency (also known as scurvy) can cause diarrhea, dental abnormalities, and skin lesions. The lack of vitamin C can cause guinea pigs to die.

    Can guinea pigs eat bananas?

    Overall, bananas can be a great treat every once in a while for healthy guinea pigs. They are great health benefits like potassium and vitamin C, both are vital for your pig's health. Bananas can also be harmful to your guinea pig if they are not properly portioned due to their high levels of sugar and fiber.
