    Channing Tatum’s Age & Early Life

    Channing Matthew Tatum was born on April 26, 1980, in Cullman, Alabama, USA. His nationality is American, while his ethnicity is English. He was born in England to Glenn Tatum (father) and Kay Tatum (mother). His father worked for an airline, while his mother worked in construction. Paige Tatum is his younger sister.

    When he was six years old, his family relocated to the Pascagoula, Mississippi region. Growing up, he struggled with attention deficit disorder (ADD) and dyslexia. He grew up playing football, soccer, track & field, baseball, and martial arts. He went to Gaither High School and Tampa Catholic High School before graduating in 1998. He then attended Glenville State College on a football scholarship in Glenville, West Virginia, but dropped out. He started working as a stripper at a local bar after skipping college.

    Career

    Tatum has been involved in the sector since 2000. In 2000, he was the first dancer cast in Ricky Martin’s “She Bangs” music video. In 2002, this actor began appearing in television ads for Mountain Dew and Pepsi. In 2004, he made his acting debut as Bob Davenport in an episode of the television series CSI: Miami. Coach Carter (2005) was his debut film appearance as Jason Lyle. His breakout role came in the 2006 dance film Step Up, which brought him to the attention of a broader public. In the film, he portrayed dancer Tyler Gage. He was named one of the “50 Most Beautiful Faces” by Tear Sheet magazine in October 2001.

    He earned the 2010 Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie Actor: Action Adventure for his performance as Conrad Hauser / Duke in G.I. Joe: The Rise of Cobra in 2009. In 2012, he starred as Michael Lane/Magic Mike in the film Magic Mike (2012), for which he received an MTV Movie Award nomination. He also reprised his character in the film’s sequel, Magic Mike XXL (2015). 21 Jump Street, White House Down, 22 Jump Street, and Jupiter Ascending are among his other well-known works.

    Channing Tatum Wife & Marriage

    Who is Channing Tatum’s wife? Channing Tatum is not in a relationship. He had an on-again, off-again romance with singer Jessie J. They began dating in March 2019 but were first seen together in 2018. However, he and his girlfriend just split up in late October 2020. According to Jessie, it was an unhealthy love.

    He was formerly married to Jenna Dewan, an American actress and singer. They married on July 11, 2009. On the sets of their film Step Up, the pair met for the first time. After a few months of dating, the couple decided to be married. The couple married in Malibu, California, three years after their romance began. In 2013, they welcomed a daughter called Everly Tatum.
