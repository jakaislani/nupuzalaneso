    Cheesecake Dip

    Dips are the perfect party food and this simple, delicious Cherry Cheesecake Dip is no exception. It starts with a creamy no bake cheesecake base that is made with cream cheese and Greek yogurt and is topped with a can of cherry pie filling. Simple and delicious.

    It’s incredibly quick to make (we’re talking less than 5 minutes) and is the perfect make-ahead sweet treat for your next gathering. This dip can be made without the pie filling on top as well and serves as the perfect fruit dip. So good!

    Not a fan of cherry? Try other pie filling flavors instead! Blueberry, apple, strawberry and lemon are all delicious!

    Why I Love This Cheesecake Dip

    Sweet and creamy with a nice tang, this easy cheesecake dip is a favorite with kids and adults alike. It’s a simple party dip that is made with ingredients that I keep on hand and takes just minutes to prep and assemble. If you’re a fan of easy, delicious appetizers like I am then you’re not going to want to miss this recipe!

    • What You’ll Need

    Only a few simple ingredients are required for this easy cheesecake dip recipe! As always, you can find the full printable recipe with complete instructions in the recipe card at the end of this post. Let’s take a look at what you’ll need:

    • cream cheese – you’ll want to make sure your cream cheese is fully softened so it combines with the other ingredients and spreads easily. I do recommend full fat cream cheese.
    • Greek yogurt – this creates a lovely creaminess to the dip and adds a tang that offsets the sweetness of the marshmallow fluff and powdered sugar.
    • powdered sugar – thickens the dip as well as sweetens.
    • lemon juice or lime juice, fresh squeezed
    • almond extract – any extract flavor can be used here, including vanilla extract. I like almond extract because it’s clear and almond and cherry go together so beautifully.
    • marshmallow fluff – I love the consistency of marshmallow fluff and it’s a great way to add fluffiness s well as sweetness to this each cheesecake dip.
    • cherry pie filling – or other flavor of your choosing.

    To Serve

    You’ll need some dippers to serve alongside this cheesecake dip. Here are a few recommendations:

    • graham crackers
    • Nilla wafers
    • fresh fruit
    • Animal crackers
    • cookies
    • broken waffle cones/cups
    • butter crisps 
    • pizzelle cookies
    • broken cannoli shells or cannoli chips

    How To Make Cherry Cheesecake Dip

    This dip comes together in just minutes! Let’s take a quick look at the easy steps:

  • Beat the cream cheese and powdered sugar together until creamy and smooth.
  • Mix in the Greek yogurt, lemon juice and almond extract until combined.
  • Add the marshmallow fluff and mix until smooth.
  • Transfer dip to a serving dish or bowl.
  • Gently spread the cherry pie filling on top.
  • Cover and chill until ready to serve.
  • Serve with graham crackers, Nilla wafers or your favorite dippers.

    Storage Information

    This cheesecake dip can be stored, tightly covered or in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. I do not recommend freezing this dip.

    Recipe Variations

    I think this recipe is perfect as is but I love to give options and variations to try to so you can really make the recipe your own. Here are some tasty ideas:

    • Try a different flavor extract to add a different flavor to the cheesecake dip. Just make sure it pairs well with the pie filling topping.
    • Try different pie filing flavors such as apple, blueberry, strawberry and lemon.
    • Create individual servings by piping or spooning the cheesecake dip into small cups or ramekins and then topping with a tablespoon or so of the pie filling. Super cute and fun for parties!

    Cherry Cheesecake Dip

    This Cherry Cheesecake Dip is extra delicious and so easy to make! Sweet and creamy, this easy cheesecake dip recipe takes just minutes to make and can be prepared ahead of time. Everyone loves the creamy no bake cheesecake and the bright red cherry topping is almost too pretty to eat! Perfect for parties!

    Course Appetizer, Dessert Cuisine American Keyword cheesecake dip, cheesecake dip recipe, cherry cheesecake dip, no bake cheesecake dip Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 10 Calories 253kcal Author Trish – Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 8 ounces cream cheese softened
    • ⅔ cup powdered sugar
    • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
    • 1 teaspoon lemon juice or lime juice, fresh squeezed
    • ½ teaspoon almond extract
    • 7 ounces marshmallow fluff
    • 21 ounces cherry pie filling

    To Serve

    • graham crackers, Nilla wafers, fresh fruit, etc.

    Instructions

    • Using a hand or stand mixer, beat the cream cheese and powdered sugar together until creamy and smooth.

      8 ounces cream cheese, ⅔ cup powdered sugar

    • Mix in the Greek yogurt, lemon juice and almond extract until completely combined.

      1 cup plain Greek yogurt, 1 teaspoon lemon juice, ½ teaspoon almond extract

    • On low speed, add the marshmallow fluff and mix until smooth. Increase speed to medium and beat until light and fluffy.

      7 ounces marshmallow fluff

    • Transfer dip to a 2 quart serving dish or bowl and smooth the surface.

    • Gently spread the cherry pie filling on top in an even layer.

      21 ounces cherry pie filling

    • Cover and chill until ready to serve. Serve with graham crackers, Nilla wafers or your favorite dippers.

      graham crackers, Nilla wafers, fresh fruit, etc.

    Notes

    Storage Information: This cheesecake dip can be stored, tightly covered or in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days. I do not recommend freezing this dip.
