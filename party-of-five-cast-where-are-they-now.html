    The Party of Five cast has continued to support each over the years since starring on the beloved drama series.

    The show — which ran for six seasons on Fox from 1994 to 2000 — followed the lives of siblings Charlie (Matthew Fox), Bailey (Scott Wolf), Julia (Neve Campbell), Claudia (Lacey Chabert) and Owen Salinger (Jacob Smith), as they learned to navigate life after their parents die in a car accident. While Party of Five was chock full of heartwarming family moments, the Salingers’ story also dealt with tough topics such as alcoholism, cancer and domestic violence.

    Party of Five also starred Jennifer Love Hewitt, Scott Grimes, Michael A. Goorjian, Paula Devicq, Jeremy London, Tom Mason and Jennifer Aspen. The series took home the 1996 Golden Globe for Best Drama Series and was nominated again for the award the following year.

    “It really had a significant impact on the people that watched it,” Wolf told HuffPost Live in February 2015. “That was something that I know I was, and all of us that made that show, were really proud of.”
