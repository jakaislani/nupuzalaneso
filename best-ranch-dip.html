    The Best Ranch Dip to Buy [Best Store Bought Ranch Dip Taste Test]

    The best ranch dip is creamy, herby, and perfect for dipping chips or veggies. It should make a great midday snack or a perfect party appetizer. Most importantly, it should have everything you love about ranch dressing in thicker, richer dip form.

    We tried the most popular, most available, and highest-rated options available at the grocery store to find the absolute best ranch dip. I love ranch dip so this taste test was very important to me. I’m happy to report we found multiple choices for the best store bought ranch dip, each with its own virtues. Get those carrot sticks ready for dipping!

    Best Creamy

    Daisy Creamy Sour Cream Ranch DipInstacart

    You probably know Daisy for their sour cream, but now they’re making dip! This is one of the best store bought ranch dip options if you want a thick and creamy dip that has a mild flavor. It’s perfect for a flavorful dipper, like seasonal cherry tomatoes or kettle cooked BBQ potato chips. This dip will complement your dipping options rather than overpower them.

    Credit: Merc / Instacart

    Rating:

    8/10

    Sporks

    Most Nostalgic

    Lay’s Smooth Ranch DipInstacart

    This is not necessarily the best ranch dip in the entire world, but it’s so deliciously nostalgic. And don’t get me wrong! It’s still delicious, especially in that processed junk food way. It’s thick, goopy, and indescribably ranch-y. I cannot pick out the garlic or dill like you can with freshly made ranch, but it still tastes like ranch! It’s super savory and salty. This is a classic dip for a reason.

    Credit: Merc / Instacart

    Rating:

    8/10

    Sporks

    Most Surprising

    Doritos Cool Ranch Jalapeño Flavored DipInstacart

    This dip is so surprisingly good. I thought it would be gimmicky. It’s not. It’s really, really good! It tastes like cool ranch Doritos in dip form. If you dipped cool ranch Doritos into this, you would send your brain straight to space. Trust us and try it.

    Credit: Merc / Instacart

    Rating:

    8.5/10

    Sporks

    Most Unique

    Taste of the South Fried Pickle and Ranch DipInstacart

    This is the best ranch dip for a plain chip because it has so much flavor. This Taste of the South dip tastes like a fried dill pickle dipped in ranch dressing. There’s so much dill, it has a nice little tang, and it’s super creamy. If you’re looking for a next-level ranch dip, this is it. It’s the best store bought ranch dip because it’s so much more than just ranch dip. Pickle girlies, this one’s for you!

    Credit: Merc / Instacart

    Rating:

    9/10

    Sporks

    Best for Veggies

    Marzetti Veggie DipInstacart

    Marzetti makes some of the best ranch dip out there for dipping fresh veggies. They obviously know it, too. This stuff is explicitly called “veggie dip.” It tastes like the best version of the ranch you get with a grocery store vegetable platter. It’s thick and perfect for scooping up with a baby carrot. Speaking of carrots, this ranch dip has carrots in it! The carrot flavor doesn’t really stand out, but they do add a nice bit of texture. Your vegetables are screaming to be dipped in Marzetti.

    Credit: Merc / Instacart

    Rating:

    9.5/10

    Sporks

    Best of the Best

    Dean’s Ranch DipUber Eats

    My boss, Gwynedd Stuart, was like, “Dean’s is the best.” I was skeptical for some reason. You know it’s the best ranch dip without tasting any other ranch dips? I owe her an apology, though. I never should have doubted her. It is the best! It tastes like making ranch with the Hidden Valley seasoning packet, except it’s thicker, richer, and there is absolutely no mixing required. If you want the best ranch dip when it comes to flavor, texture, and richness, you absolutely have to go with Dean’s.

    Credit: Merc / Uber Eats

    Rating:

    10/10

    Sporks
