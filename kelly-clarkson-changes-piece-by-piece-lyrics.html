    How Kelly Clarkson Changed the Lyrics to &quot;Piece by Piece&quot;

    Kelly Clarkson wrote &quot;Piece by Piece&quot; about her relationship with Brandon Blackstock before they hit a rough patchand she just performed the song for the first time since their split. And yep, some lyrical changes have most certainly been made.

    22 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Kelly Clarkson wrote "Piece by Piece" about her relationship with Brandon Blackstock before they hit a rough patch—and she just performed the song for the first time since their split. And yep, some lyrical changes have most certainly been made.

    Speaking to her audience in Las Vegas, Clarkson said, “This song I initially wrote just super hopeful right? And well, sometimes hopeful turns into hopeless, so here we go. 'Piece by Piece.'"

    Related Story

    What changes were made to the song? Well, this is the old chorus (via Genius):

    "But piece by piece, he collected me
    Up off the ground, where you abandoned things, yeah
    Piece by piece, he filled the holes
    That you burned in me at six years old
    And you know
    He never walks away, he never asks for money
    He takes care of me, he loves me
    Piece by piece, he restored my faith
    That a man can be kind and a father could
    Stay"

    The new lyrics?

    "Piece by piece, I collected me
    Off the ground where you abandoned things
    Piece by piece, I filled the holes
    That you burned in me at six years old
    I just walk away when they ask for money
    I take care of me, 'cause I love me
    Piece by piece, I restored my faith
    That a heart can still beat even when it breaks"

    Clarkson also made tweaks to the final chorus of the song, which originally went:

    "Piece by piece, I fell far from the tree
    I will never leave him like you left me
    And she will never have to wonder her worth
    Because unlike you, I'm gonna put her first
    And you know
    He'll never walk away, he'll never break her heart
    He'll take care of things, he'll love her
    Piece by piece, he restored my faith
    That a man can be kind and a father should be great"

    The new version goes:

    "Piece by piece, I fell far from the tree
    I will never leave them like you left me
    And they will never have to wonder their worth
    Because unlike you, I’m gonna put them first
    I never walk away, I never break their hearts
    I’ll take care of things when you leave scars
    Piece by piece, I restored my faith
    That a heart can still beat even if it breaks"
