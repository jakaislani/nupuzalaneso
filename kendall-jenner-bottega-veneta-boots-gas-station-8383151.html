    Kendall Jenner Pumps Her Gas in $10K Bottega Veneta Boots

    If youve ever arrived at the gas station looking less than great (guilty!), look away now, because like most things we mere mortals experience on a daily basis, Kendall Jenner simply cant relate.

    17 Jun 2024, 00:00

    If you’ve ever arrived at the gas station looking less than great (guilty!), look away now, because like most things we mere mortals experience on a daily basis, Kendall Jenner simply can’t relate.

    On Wednesday, the A-lister decided to show everyone up by arriving at a Los Angeles gas station wearing all-designer everything. While the discovery that Kendall pumps her own gas (well, when there are paparazzi shots to be had, at least) was certainly one of the most shocking parts of her outing, it was topped only by her choice of outfit: a navy blue collared midi dress and green-and-yellow striped Bottega Veneta knee-high boots, which boast an eye-watering $10,000 price tag.

    Black square-framed sunglasses, simple silver earrings, and a folded magazine accessorized the model’s super-cinched button-up frock and BV Canalazzo boots, and she wore her brunette hair down in soft waves with a middle part. Although there’s no denying that Jenner looked positively stunning during the outing, staged or not, it goes to show that sometimes, celebrities aren’t just like us after all.

    On a much more relatable note, Jenner’s outing came just days after she sported a much more replicable look when stepping out in support of her maybe-boyfriend, Bad Bunny, at the Saturday Night Live after-party. This time opting for comfort over style, Kendall slipped into a slouchy gray suede jacket, which she layered over a plain black T-shirt and matching black pants and loafers. The Kardashians star once again styled her hair down in loose waves with a middle part, and she added black polish to her nails to round out her East Coast, (almost) all-black look.
