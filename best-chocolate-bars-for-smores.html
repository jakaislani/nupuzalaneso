    The best chocolate bars for s’mores are sweet, meltable, and, most importantly, can be easily broken into a square.

    It’s one thing to rank chocolate bars—but ranking chocolate bars for s’mores is a whole other ballgame. We tried so many different flavors, types, shapes, and textures of chocolate bars to find out which are best to combine with marshmallows, graham crackers, and an open fire.

    Some were tasty and some totally flopped, but we selected these seven as the absolute best chocolate bars for s’mores that you can buy. Don’t believe me? Go camping and try them yourself! Grab your marshmallows and graham crackers and get ready for this list.

    The following article contains affiliate links that may generate a small commission to us when you make a purchase through the link. Learn more about how we work with affiliates here.

    Best of the Best

    Ghirardelli Milk Chocolate Caramel SquaresTarget

    Ghirardelli Milk Chocolate Caramel Squares are so decadent and luxurious. They feel like they should cost $100. Luckily, they don’t—they’re very affordable and come in a big bag. These chocolate squares are great on their own, but when added to a s’more, they’re transcendent. The melty caramel and rich chocolate with the marshmallow and graham crackers is pure perfection. Plus, these are exactly the right size to fit on a s’more. It feels like they were destined for s’mores greatness from the start!

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Target

    Rating:

    10/10

    Best Dark Chocolate

    Theo Dark Chocolate Sea Salt Chocolate BarInstacart

    In my opinion, if you’re going for dark chocolate, you need to go salty. Theo Dark Chocolate agrees! This Dark Chocolate Sea Salt bar is definitely the best chocolate bar for s’mores if you’re a dark chocolate lover. The flavor is deep without being bitter. There’s a perfect amount of salt. This is what you should reach for if you want to make a more mature version of a classic s’more. Not that the original isn’t great, but sometimes you just want to feel like an adult.

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Instacart

    Rating:

    10/10

    Sporks

    Best Texture

    Trader Joe’s Milk Chocolate Crispy Rice Chocolate BarTraderjoes.com

    The Trader Joe’s Milk Chocolate Crispy Rice Chocolate Bar is basically a better version of the beloved Nestle Crunch Bar. The puffed rice has a wonderful texture and the chocolate is creamy and sweet. These come in multipacks and are super affordable, so you get a good bang for your buck. These aren’t just the best chocolate bars for s’mores—they’re one of the best chocolate bars in general.

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Amazon

    Rating:

    10/10

    Sporks

    Best Milk Chocolate

    Sprout’s Organic Milk Chocolate BarInstacart

    This was the creamiest, milkiest milk chocolate bar we tried. The texture is so smooth and dreamy. It’s sweet without tasting too artificial. It’s organic, which is a nice added bonus. Plus, it’s affordable because it’s Sprouts brand! If you’re a s’mores purist, this is the bar you should be placing inbetween your marshmallows and graham crackers.

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Instacart

    Rating:

    9.5/10

    Sporks

    Best Milk Runner-Up

    Hershey’s Milk Chocolate BarTarget

    The moment you pull out a Hershey’s Milk Chocolate bar, everyone suddenly becomes British. They will say things like, “I hate American chocolates,” and, “Ugh, it’s just so sweet.” Please drop the act, choco-traitors. Hershey’s Milk Chocolate Bars are delicious. And more importantly, they are definitely one of the best chocolate bars for s’mores. No other milk chocolate bar we tried had a better melt factor, breakability, or shape. Hershey’s is a classic for a reason, people!

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Target

    Rating:

    9/10

    Most Fun

    Fruity Pebbles Chocolate BarWalmartAmazon

    Fruity Pebbles products are incredible. Seriously! Every single one we’re tried, we’ve loved. The original cereal? Perfect. The Easter candy? Sensational. And their chocolate bar? Easily one of the best chocolate bars for s’mores. The texture is crunchy and creamy. It’s a little fruity, super sweet, and tastes so dang nostalgic. If you’re looking for a fun s’more that kids (and adults!) would absolutely love, grab some Fruity Pebbles Chocolate Bars for your next camping trip.

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Walmart

    Rating:

    8.5/10

    Most Unique

    Chocolove Chili Mole Chocolate BarIntacart

    From the taste to the name, everything about the Chocolove Chili Mole Chocolate Bar is sexy. If you’re looking for a cool grown-up s’more that would impress your most culinarily adventurous friend, this is the bar for you. It’s spicy and it has an interesting crunch that will add textural nuance to your s’more from the addition of espresso beans, pistachios, and apricots in the bar. The Chocolove Chili Mole Chocolate Bar is the best chocolate bar to use if you’re serving s’mores at a fancy cocktail party.

    Credit: Sarah Demonteverde / Instacart

    Rating:

    8/10

    Best White Chocolate

    Lindt White Chocolate BarTarget

    I am not a white chocolate fan, but I cannot deny that the Lindt White Chocolate Bar is great. This would definitely be a white chocolate lover’s pick for the best chocolate bar for s’mores. It was easily the best pure white chocolate bar we tried. It melts well and is super sweet. And, if you can believe it, this white chocolate bar was the best bar we had from Lindt in general. Take note, Lindt lovers!
