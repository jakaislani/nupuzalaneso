    Judah Akers Judah and Lion Band

    Akers is a vastly recognized rock singer and guitarist. He is well known as the lead singer of rock band Judah & the Lion. Moreover, their music comes from many influences that cumulates in a blend of folk instruments.

    It is known that Judah and his band are known for their 2016 single “Take It All Back.” Likewise, the band supported Twenty-One Pilots on the Emotional Roadshow tour led by Tyler Joseph in 2017.

    Most noteworthy, Akers met Banjo player Nate Zuercher and mandolinist Brian Macdonald through mutual friends. Afterward, he started playing music with them in late 2011. Prior, they aligned with the Christian music scene. They made their debut EP, First Fruits as a worship-themed record that blended expansive folk.

    Afterward, the band released a more secular-oriented and pop-leaning Sweet Tennessee EP. As a result, their nu-folk of Mumford & Sons launched them into the Top 20 of the Billboard bluegrass and folk charts.
