    Dancing with the Stars is feeling bejeweled. The hit ABC series will be having A Celebration of Taylor Swift on November 21. The 6 remaining couples will dance to the *gorgeous* hits of the one and only Taylor Swift.

    14 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Dancing with the Stars is feeling bejeweled. The hit ABC series will be having A Celebration of Taylor Swift on November 21. The 6 remaining couples will dance to the *gorgeous* hits of the one and only Taylor Swift.

    Mandy Moore, Dancing with the Stars choreographer and the lead choreographer of The Eras Tour, will be a special guest judge on Taylor Swift Night.

    When Is Taylor Swift Night on ‘DWTS’?

    A Celebration of Taylor Swift will air at 8 p.m. on November 21. The episode will be simulcast live across both ABC and Disney+. At the end of the episode, another couple will be sent home. Season 32 is inching closer to the upcoming finale, which is expected to air on December 5.

    Will Taylor Swift Be on ‘DWTS’?

    Taylor will not be making an in-person appearance during the DWTS episode dedicated to her. The singer has a concert in Rio De Janeiro, Brazil, on November 20, which means that she won’t be attending Travis Kelce’s Kansas City Chiefs game against the Philadelphia Eagles. Taylor has another concert in São Paulo, Brazil, on November 24. 

    During the November 14 episode of DWTS, Taylor made a surprise appearance via video. “I can’t wait to see Dancing with the Stars’ celebration of my Eras next week,” Taylor said. “I wish I could be there with you guys but I’m on tour in Brazil, but I will be there in spirit and I’ll be watching.”

    What Will the Couples Dance to on Taylor Swift Night?

    ABC has not announced exactly which Taylor Swift songs the remaining pairs will be dancing to on Taylor Swift Night. The remaining couples include Jason Mraz and Daniella Karagach, Xochitl Gomez and Val Chmerkovskiy, Ariana Madix and Pasha Pashkov, Alyson Hannigan and Sasha Farber, Harry Jowsey and Rylee Arnold, and Charity Lawson and Artem Chigvintsev.

    In the days leading up to the Taylor Swift-centered episode, the couples have teased their dances and given possible hints as to the song choices. Jason and Charity will be dancing Argentine tangos with their partners, while Harry and Ariana will be taking on rumbas. Alyson and Sasha will dance a cha cha, while Xochitl and Val will dance a quickstep.

    The celebs will also be facing off against other couples in relay dances. The relay dance face-offs include Harry and Rylee vs. Alyson and Sasha, Ariana and Pasha vs. Xochitl and Val, and Charity and Artem vs. Jason and Daniella.
