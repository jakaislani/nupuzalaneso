    When Is the Optimum Time to Trim Monkey Grass?

     Just as with other varieties of ornamental grasses, the best time to trim monkey grass is in the late winter or early spring. This manner, the monkey grass will remain green throughout the winter and will protect its roots from the cold; after cutting, the grass will only be bald for a few weeks while new leaves form.
